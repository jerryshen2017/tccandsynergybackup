Clean And Secure



Street Synergy’s daily efforts to keep the neighborhood clean; entails promoting individual resident responsibility through the Block Clubs and a close relationship with the Mayor’s Impact Team for the bigger jobs. Also requires SSCA to maintain strong working contacts with E District Police.




Better On Bailey




SSCA’s commercial redevelopment effort, in concert with UB, NY State and Buffalo’s Office of Strategic Planning. A revitalized commercial strip along Bailey is critical to neighborhood revitalization.



Urban Discovery




A long running program created by SSCA to promote home ownership in the community, in collaboration with local banks, businesses, schools and real estate companies. The 2008 event was held at Highgate Elementary School.
