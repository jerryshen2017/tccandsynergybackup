Who Are We?



Street Synergy began as a merger of five community block clubs. These same neighbors,plus many more, formed Street Synergy as a group of dedicated city residents, on a volunteer basis, to serve themselves.

Street Synergy is an organization of social change, and, as such, addresses the root causes of urban problems, not the consequences. We are striving to improve our community through our programs: Clean and Secure, Better on Bailey, and Urban Discovery
